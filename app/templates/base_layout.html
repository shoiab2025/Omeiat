About Us

Organization of Muslim Educational Institutions & Associations of Tamil Nadu (OMEIAT).

Established in 1974, OMEIAT unites Muslim educational institutions across Tamil Nadu to promote quality education, protect minority rights, and empower educators and students for a brighter future.
